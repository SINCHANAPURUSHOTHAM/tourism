The History of Mykonos

Explore the rich history and beauty of this iconic Greek island

3000 BCE

First Settlements

The first signs of human habitation on Mykonos date back to the Early Bronze Age, with evidence of settlements along the island's coast.

5th Century BCE

Mykonos in the Classical Era

Mykonos played a supporting role in the naval battles of the Classical Greek period, and its wealth grew due to maritime trade.

1204 CE

Venetian Rule

After the Fourth Crusade, Mykonos came under Venetian rule, which led to significant architectural and cultural influences still visible today.

1821 CE

Greek War of Independence

Mykonos played a key role in the Greek War of Independence, with its population actively participating in the fight against Ottoman rule.

1950s-60s

Tourism Boom

In the mid-20th century, Mykonos experienced a tourism boom, becoming one of the most popular destinations in Greece. The island's picturesque landscapes, vibrant nightlife, and rich cultural heritage attracted visitors from around the world, transforming the local economy and shaping the island's modern identity.
