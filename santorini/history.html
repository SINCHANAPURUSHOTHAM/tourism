The History of Santorini

Explore the rich and turbulent past of this stunning island.

3000 BCE

Minoan Civilization

The island was part of the Minoan civilization, known for its advanced culture, trade, and impressive architecture, including the ruins of Akrotiri.

1620 BCE

Volcanic Eruption

A massive volcanic eruption reshaped the island, leading to the caldera formation and the destruction of the Minoan settlements on Santorini.

9th Century BCE

Greek Colonization

After the eruption, the island was re-inhabited by Dorians, leading to the establishment of new settlements and the spread of Greek culture.

1204 CE

Venetian Rule

Following the Fourth Crusade, Santorini came under Venetian control, which influenced the island's architecture and culture significantly.

1821 CE

Greek War of Independence

During the Greek War of Independence, Santorini played a role in the struggle against Ottoman rule, contributing to the movement for Greek independence.
