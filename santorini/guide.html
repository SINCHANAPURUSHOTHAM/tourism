Santorini Travel Guide

Your complete guide for exploring the beautiful Santorini

Getting to Santorini

By Air

Santorini (Thira) Airport is the main access point, located about 6 km from Fira.

  • Direct flights from major European cities
  • Seasonal flights from other international locations

By Sea

Regular ferries connect Santorini with other Greek islands and mainland Greece.

  • Ferries from Athens (Piraeus) take about 5-8 hours
  • Fast ferries are available for quicker transfers

Where to Stay

Recommended Areas

  • Fira - Central hub with shops and restaurants
  • Oia - Famous for stunning sunsets and luxury hotels
  • Kamari - Beachfront area with a relaxed vibe
  • Perissa - Known for its black sand beach

Accommodation Types

Type Price Range (€/night)
Budget Hostels 20-50
Mid-range Hotels 80-200
Luxury Hotels 250+

Getting Around Santorini

Bus Services

Santorini has a reliable bus system connecting major towns and beaches.

  • Regular services between Fira, Oia, Kamari, and Perissa
  • Tickets: €1.80 per ride

Car & Scooter Rentals

Renting a car or scooter is a popular option for exploring the island at your own pace.

  • Rental prices range from €30-€70 per day
  • International driving permit recommended

Suggested Itinerary

3-Day Itinerary

Experience the best of Santorini in just three days!

  • Day 1: Explore Fira, visit the Archaeological Museum, and take a sunset cruise.
  • Day 2: Visit Oia for stunning views, explore the local shops, and enjoy a wine tasting tour.
  • Day 3: Relax at Kamari beach, visit ancient Akrotiri, and enjoy a traditional Greek dinner.

Tips for Visitors

  • Book accommodations early, especially during peak season.
  • Wear comfortable shoes for walking on cobblestone streets.
  • Carry water and sunscreen, especially during summer.
