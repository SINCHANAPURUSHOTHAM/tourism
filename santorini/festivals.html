Cultural Festivals in Santorini

Experience the vibrant celebrations of Santorini's culture and traditions

August

Santorini Wine Festival

A celebration of the island's renowned wine heritage featuring tastings, local food, and music in the village of Pyrgos.

Location: Pyrgos Village
Price: Free entry
Highlights: Wine tastings and traditional music
July

Fira's Feast of the Assumption

A religious and cultural celebration with a grand procession, traditional dances, and delicious local food held in the capital of Santorini.

Location: Fira
Price: Free
Highlights: Procession and local cuisine
September

Ancient Thira Festival

A cultural festival held at the archaeological site of Ancient Thira, featuring performances and exhibitions highlighting the island's history.

Location: Ancient Thira
Price: Entry fee to archaeological site
Highlights: Cultural performances and exhibitions
